About City Library

Serving our community with knowledge and imagination since 1950

Our Mission

At City Library, our mission is to inspire lifelong learning, advance knowledge, and strengthen our community. We strive to:

  • Provide free and open access to information and ideas
  • Foster a love of reading and learning
  • Embrace diversity and promote inclusivity
  • Offer innovative programs and services
  • Preserve and share our community's history

Our History

Founded in 1950, City Library has been a cornerstone of our community for over seven decades. What started as a small reading room has grown into a modern, multi-branch system serving thousands of patrons each year.

Throughout our history, we've adapted to changing times and technologies while maintaining our commitment to free and equal access to information. From our first bookmobile in the 1960s to our digital collections today, we've continually evolved to meet the needs of our diverse community.

Today, City Library stands as a testament to the power of community support and the enduring value of public libraries in the digital age.
